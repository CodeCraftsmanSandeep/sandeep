🔍 Health Monitoring System for Surveillance Cameras
🛠️ Developed a Python-based automated monitoring system to track the status of surveillance cameras across various models (📷 Panasonic, 🎥 Sony, etc.). The system sends queries to camera IP addresses and provides real-time updates to ISRO personnel, playing a crucial role in rocket launches 🚀 and island surveillance 🏝️. Heartbeat testing.

⏳ Previously, camera checks were done manually. This new automated system runs periodic health checks (e.g., every minute ⏱️), sends queries, and analyzes responses, significantly reducing manual effort and improving operational efficiency.
