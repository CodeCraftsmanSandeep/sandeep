♻️ Sustainability Project

Ever imagined converting sound energy 🔊 into electricity ⚡? While we’re all familiar with solar ☀️, wind 🌬️, and hydro 💧, sound is an untapped powerhouse waiting to be harnessed!

I developed an acoustic energy harvester that captures everyday sounds, amplifies them via resonant design, and transforms them into electrical energy. It’s a fusion of art and science that challenges conventional thinking and sparks innovation!
